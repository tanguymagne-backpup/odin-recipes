Gaspacho

Description

Gaspacho is a refreshing tomato soup from Spain, served cold. It very nice to drink in the summer.

Preparation

It requires 15 minutes of preparation and no cooking.

It is a very easy recipe. Simply wash and cut all vegetables in small pieces. Put everything in a bowl, add some olive oil, some vinegar, salt and pepper. Then blend everything smoothly, put it in the fridge for some time and it's ready.

Ingredients

For 6 persons

  • 1 onion
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 9 tomatoes
  • 1 cucumbers
  • some fresk basil
  • salt & pepper
  • olive oil & vinegar
