Croziflette

Description

Croziflette is a delicious winter meal eaten in the french alps. It is mainly made of cheese and crozet, which are a kind of pasta.

Preparation

It requires 10 minutes of preparation and 40 minutes of cooking.

You can first cook the crozet for 20 minutes in salty and boiling water. In the mean time, cook the onion for 10 minutes with butter, and then the bacon. In a plate put the crozet, the bacon, the onion and the cream. On top of it put the sliced reblochon. Make everything brown on the oven and you are good to go.

Ingredients

For 4 persons

  • 1 reblochon
  • 300g of crozet
  • 1 onion
  • 20cl of cream
  • 200g of bacon
  • salt & pepper
