Chocolate Cake

Description

This is the recipe for a delicious chocolate cake to share with friends.

Preparation

It requires 10 minutes of preparation and 30 minutes of cooking.

You can first mix the sugar, the eggs and the flour. Then, the idea is too let the chocolate and butter melt, and add it to the original mixture.

Ingredients

For 6 persons

  • 100g of sugar
  • 50g of flour
  • 100g of butter
  • 200g of chocolate
  • 3 eggs
